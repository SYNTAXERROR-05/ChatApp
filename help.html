Getting Started

How do I create a FutureChat account?

Creating a FutureChat account is simple:

  1. Click the "Get Started" button on the homepage
  2. Choose "Sign Up" and fill in your details
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile setup
  5. Start chatting with friends and colleagues!
Is FutureChat free to use?

Yes! FutureChat offers a comprehensive free tier that includes:

  • Unlimited messaging
  • Voice and video calls
  • File sharing up to 100MB
  • Basic themes and customization

Premium features are available with our Pro subscription.

Messaging

How do I send voice messages?

Sending voice messages is easy:

  1. Open a chat conversation
  2. Press and hold the microphone button
  3. Speak your message
  4. Release to send, or slide to cancel

Voice messages are automatically enhanced with noise reduction technology.

Can I translate messages in real-time?

Yes! FutureChat includes AI-powered real-time translation:

  • Automatic language detection
  • Support for 100+ languages
  • Instant translation with high accuracy
  • Translation badges show when messages are translated

Enable auto-translation in Settings > Language.

Privacy & Security

How secure are my conversations?

Your privacy is our top priority:

  • End-to-end encryption: Only you and the recipient can read messages
  • Zero-knowledge architecture: We can't access your conversations
  • Secure servers: Military-grade security infrastructure
  • Regular audits: Third-party security assessments
Can I delete messages after sending?

Yes, you have full control over your messages:

  • Delete for yourself only
  • Delete for everyone (within 24 hours)
  • Edit messages (within 15 minutes)
  • Set disappearing messages timer

Settings & Customization

How do I change themes?

Customize your FutureChat experience:

  1. Click the theme toggle in the top bar
  2. Or go to Settings > Appearance
  3. Choose from Light, Dark, or Auto modes
  4. Customize accent colors (Pro feature)
How do I manage notifications?

Control your notification preferences:

  • Go to Settings > Notifications
  • Enable/disable push notifications
  • Set quiet hours
  • Customize notification sounds
  • Choose notification preview level
